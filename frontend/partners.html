Partnerships & Supporters

Stronger Together: Collaborating for Youth Empowerment

Caverra World believes in the power of collaboration. Our partnerships with like-minded organizations, government agencies, and dedicated individuals are fundamental to expanding our reach and deepening our impact.

Featured Partner: The OCI Foundation

A Deep-Rooted Partnership for Health & Empowerment

Our collaboration with the OCI Foundation is a cornerstone of Caverra World's outreach. The OCI Foundation is renowned for its impactful initiatives, particularly in health advocacy and youth development.

ArOY Health Campaign: Through this partnership, we integrate the OCI Foundation's "Arm Our Youths (ArOY) Health Campaign" into our programs. This vital campaign focuses on educating young people about preventative healthcare, especially against cancer and other debilitating diseases, thereby empowering them with knowledge for healthier lives.

Teenage Faces: We also work closely on initiatives like "Teenage Faces," which aims to highlight and celebrate the positive aspects of adolescence, encouraging self-esteem and healthy growth among Nigerian youth.

As the Lagos State Champion for the OCI Foundation, our founder Mrs. Roseline Ify Anijekwu ensures seamless integration and maximum impact of these crucial programs within Caverra World's activities.

Caverra Man/Woman of the Year Award

Recognizing exceptional individuals who inspire and empower our youth.

The Caverra Man/Woman of the Year Award is our way of celebrating distinguished personalities who have significantly contributed to youth development, mentorship, and community upliftment. These individuals embody the spirit of excellence and selfless service that Caverra World champions.

Dr. Chris Ifediora

2022 Caverra Man of the Year

Dr. Chris Ifediora, the Founder of OCI Foundation, was honored for his profound commitment to humanitarian services, particularly his groundbreaking work in cancer prevention advocacy among youths through the ArOY Health Campaign. His dedication has saved countless lives and inspired many.
