Our Programs & Events

National Teenagers' Day

National Teenagers' Day is Caverra World's flagship annual event dedicated to celebrating, inspiring, and empowering teenagers across Nigeria. This day serves as a powerful platform to recognize the unique potential of young people, providing them with invaluable insights, mentorship, and a sense of community.

History & OCI Foundation Support: Initiated with a vision to create a national movement for youth empowerment, National Teenagers' Day has grown exponentially thanks to the crucial support and partnership of organizations like the OCI Foundation. Their commitment to youth development, particularly through initiatives like the Arm Our Youths (ArOY) Health Campaign, aligns perfectly with our goals, allowing us to reach more teens with comprehensive programs.

  • Inspiring Keynote Speakers
  • Interactive Workshops & Skill Sessions
  • Career Guidance & Mentorship
  • Talent Showcases & Fun Activities
  • Health Awareness Campaigns (in partnership with OCI)

Teens' Hangout Sessions

Our Teens' Hangout Sessions provide safe, informal, and highly engaging environments where young people can connect, share, learn, and grow. These sessions are designed to foster peer relationships, facilitate open discussions on topics relevant to teenagers, and offer a relaxed alternative to formal learning.

What Happens: Each hangout is curated around themes like personal development, mental wellness, digital literacy, creative expression, or simply social bonding. We incorporate interactive games, group discussions, guest speakers, and skill-sharing activities. It's a place where teens can truly be themselves.

Past Session Summaries:

  • "Navigating Social Media & Self-Esteem" - Focused on responsible digital citizenship and body image.
  • "Future Pathways: Exploring Non-Traditional Careers" - Exposed teens to diverse career options beyond traditional fields.
  • "Stress Management & Resilience Building" - Taught practical techniques for coping with academic and social pressures.

The "Discover, Maximize, Soar!" (DMS) Book

Unlock Your Potential: A Guide for Every Teenager

By Mrs. Roseline Ify Anijekwu

The "Discover, Maximize, Soar!" (DMS) book is an essential companion for every teenager seeking to navigate the complexities of adolescence with purpose and confidence. Authored by our very own founder, Mrs. Roseline Ify Anijekwu, this book is a treasure trove of wisdom, practical advice, and empowering insights.

What You'll Find Inside:

  • Guidance on self-discovery and understanding personal strengths.
  • Strategies for maximizing academic potential and extracurricular talents.
  • Tools for setting ambitious goals and achieving them.
  • Tips for building resilience, managing emotions, and fostering healthy relationships.
  • Inspiration to make a positive impact on your community and beyond.
